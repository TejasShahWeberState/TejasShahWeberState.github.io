Favorite Sports Team



Here are my Favorite Sports Team (college and professional):

  • Utah Jazz

    Even though I was very young to know that the Utah Jazz have been in the finals twice during my lifetime, I don't remember much about them, and I became a fan during the 2000-2001 season, when I could understand basketball. John Stockton is my favorite Jazz player of all-time, but I only remembered the highlights of Stockton when he was getting up their in age. They have been good-great during my lifetime, but one day, I hope they can finally win one.

  • Utah Utes

    When it comes to college sports, I cheer for the University of Utah before anyone else. I first started the football team during 2002-2003 season, where we went 5-6 and missed a bowl. Since then, they have been a great football program, including 2 BCS Bowl wins, 1 over Pittsburgh, and 1 over Alabama. Also an appearance for a PAC-12 title game in 2018-19 doesn't hurt, either.

    When it comes to college basketball, I started watching them during the 2004-05 season, where I saw Andrew Bogut be amazing. As a college basketball program, we have been decent, but we could be better. They, unfortunately, don't have any huge wins that I can recall since I started watching them, unlike the football team.

  • Philadelphia Flyers

    They have been my favorite NHL team for a long time. They were the team I used a lot in the Nintendo 64 NHL Blades of Steel '99. I got attached to them since. I have yet to see this team to win the Stanley Cup, and while they have advanced to the Stanley Cup twice during my lifetime, I only remember the one against the Chicago BlackHawks. They lost in 6 games, ending in overtime, but they haven't come close since.

  • Arizona Cardinals

    I have been a fan of the Arizona Cardinals since the end of the 2003-04 season, when they knocked the Vikings out of the playoffs. Became a fan because of Anquan Boldin and Adrian Wilson. The next year, we got Larry Fitzgerald, and since then, he has become my favorite player. I was heartbroken when they lost to the Steelers in 2008-09 Super Bowl, and haven't gotten back since. Came close in 2015-16 before they got throttled by Carolina Panthers. I have been really disappointed by the 2018-19 Arizona Cardinals.
