  • AP Stats

    Year: 2013, Senior Year, 12th grade, Hillcrest High School

    This was my favorite high school class, by far, even with other good-great high school classes that I had. I know that while I got to watch Moneyball (great movie) and play Mario Kart Wii (great game that I was top in the class rankings), I loved it for other reasons. I had to do those after the AP test (got a 3 on it, by the way), but the class was enjoyable from the first day I had this class.

    First off, I loved this class because I felt that I got along with a lot of students in that class, and everyone was very lively during the class. We usually had 5 minute breaks to eat food, as a member of class would usually bring in treats. During those 5-10 minutes breaks, we took the time to discuss with other class members and the teacher about the subject (like which part we are stuck on) and other things.

    Second off, the material taught was interesting. Correlation, causation, biasness, proof or lack of proof, etc. where all interesting materials that I want to learn more about. And even though it was an early class, this class would make me stay awake.
